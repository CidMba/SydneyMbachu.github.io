Mentorem Academy is a non-profit organization [501(c)3 pending] with the mission of building strong and resilient learners so they can succeed no matter what school they are attending, what zip code they belong to, or what economic neighborhood they are living in.

A range of factors have been shown to increase a student’s risk of dropping out, including high rates of absenteeism, low levels of school engagement, low parental education, work or family responsibilities, problematic or deviant behavior.

This is why we plan to change the way children learn when they aren’t in school. This will help with their engagement, learning skills, and problem solving. These are the intangible skills that will last a lifetime.

Why do we need Mentorem Academy

Good question, we are glad you asked!
I think these statistics will be shocking for you.
Only 20% of a child’s waking hours are spent in school (Miller, 1995). Each weekday afternoon, at least 8 million “latchkey” children are left alone and unsupervised (Department of Education, 2002).

Why is this important

The highest crime rate during the week is from 3:00-7:00 p.m. No child will be left behind when quality after school programs are available in every neighborhood and all children have the same safe, nurturing, enriching, and character-building opportunities.

It is hard to believe but “every year, over 1.2 million students drop out of high school in the United States alone. That’s a student every 26 seconds — or 7,000 a day.” — DoSomething.org.

Why does this matter

The U.S. Census Bureau estimates that dropouts bring in just $20,241 annually, which is $10,000 less than high school graduates and over $36,000 less than a person holding a bachelor’s degree.  

"Over 80 percent of the incarcerated population are high school dropouts – making this an issue that truly impacts every member of the community." - TheEdAdvocate.org. A study out of Northeastern University found that high school dropouts cost taxpayers $292,000 over the course of their lives.

Change isn’t always easy. But it is always worth it.

Our Coaching

Here are some components of our program

Here are some components of our program

Our coaches will work with the kids parents and guardian to implement a good structure, learning environment, and habits at home like:

  • Setting a study time after school
  • Setting a reading time before bed
  • Setting a consistent bed time based on hours of sleep needed based on research

Our coaches will work with kids on mastering basic math using games like Zeus vs Monsters:

  1. Addition
  2. Subtraction
  3. Multiplication
  4. Division
  5. Multiples

After mastering their basic math skills, our coaches will work with kids on next years math curriculum using free resources like Khan Academy and Math-Aids.

All of the work that the coaches do is based on research for learning, memory, and sleep. While using sports psychology from individual and team sports to work on their intrinsic motivations.

Mentorem Academy is designed to be plugged in to any community.

We hire people from your community that understands your culture and neighborhood idiosyncrasies. We would work with you to find someone that fits both of our requirements. We hire them to be part of our team so we can hold them 100% accountable and to serve as the bridge between the students, parents/guardian, your community and us.

Ideal coach qualifications are people that has a degree, veteran (for their discipline), young certified teachers, and retirees with relevant experience. It all depends on how many students there are for each location.

We work with the coaches everyday via group web conference meetings (like Zoom) to train them, keep track of their progress, and work with the coaches to identify bottle necks and find solutions.
